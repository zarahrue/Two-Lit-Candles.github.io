The Ultimate Modest Fashion Shopping Guide

Clothing, Headscarves, + Wigs!

Modesty has many forms. Depending on your culture and community the practical, "what to wear" side of modesty can differ. As I've said before biblically modesty is about establishing boundaries and maintaining privacy. Some women prefer to keep measurement boundaries and other women don't. Below I've listed a few online stores that I believe have something for every modesty standard out there. I've included wigs because in many Jewish communities and messianic Jewish communities wigs are worn instead of headscarves in order to keep the natural hair that grows from a woman's head private.
