A Great Vegan Sweet Potato Pie Recipe!


This is a recipe that I use that was adapted from Makinze Gore. I also use pre-made dough for the crust but you may use your favorite crust recipe or store-bought crust.

The Yummy Filling:


								
  • 1 can of sweet potato purée
    • 
								
  • ¼ cup of almond milk (or dairy free milk of choice)
    • 
								
  • ¾ cups dark brown sugar
    • 
								
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch (I add another ½ tsp)
    • 
								
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
    • 
								
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 
								
  • ½ tsp kosher salt
    • 
								
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
    • 
								
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
    • 
								
  • 1 tbsp vegan butter
    •

    Baking Instructions (Super Easy)

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit and grease a 9 inch pie plate (or whatever you have) with a cooking spray. Place dough in pie plate. In a large bowl whisk together filling ingredients then pour over crust and bake only until it is slightly jiggly in the middle. Bake for 1 hour to 1 hour and 5 minutes. When done, let the pie cool and then place in refrigerator.
