Orthodox Books Everyone Should Read


If you are interested in Orthodoxy; these are excellent books to be used as resources in learning more about the Ancient Faith. I’ve read each of these (with the exception of the Orthodox Study Bible, I’ve not read the entire translation as of yet). 


								
    
    						 	
  1. St. Tikhon’s Monastery Orthodox Christian Prayer Book
										
  2. Know The Faith: A Handbook for Orthodox Christians and Inquirers by Rev. Michael Shanbour
											
  3. The Orthodox Way: Revised Edition by Bishop Kallistos Ware
												
  4. The Orthodox Church by Timothy Ware (Bishop Kallistos Ware)
													
  5. Orthdoxy and the Religion of the Future by Fr. Seraphim Rose
														
  6. Facing East: A Pilgrim’s Journey Into The Mysteries of Orthodoxy by Frederica Mathewes-Green
															
  7. Becoming Orthodox by Peter Gillquist
																
  8. Writings of St. John of Damascus
																	
  9. Orthodox Study Bible
																		
  10. On the Cosmic Mystery of Jesus Christ
